# Day 16 – Build Your First Stock Research Skill

## Objective

Learned how to create a reusable Custom Skill in Claude to perform stock fundamental analysis without rewriting prompts every time.

---

## Custom Skill

Skill Name:
stock-fundamental-research

---

## What I Did

- Created a Custom Skill in Claude.
- Configured the skill with stock research instructions.
- Tested the skill using Tata Consultancy Services (TCS).
- Generated a detailed stock analysis report.
- Reviewed valuation metrics, financial health, strengths, risks, and charts.

---

## Features Generated

- Company Overview
- Market Capitalization
- P/E Ratio
- P/B Ratio
- 52-Week High & Low
- Revenue & Profit
- Promoter Holding
- Growth Analysis
- Strengths
- Risk Factors
- Stock Dashboard
- Visual Charts

---

## Key Learnings

- Custom Skills save time by eliminating repetitive prompts.
- AI can automate stock research using reusable workflows.
- Financial metrics help evaluate company performance.
- Visual dashboards make analysis easier to understand.

---

## Tools Used

- Claude AI
- Claude Custom Skills
