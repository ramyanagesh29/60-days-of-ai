# Day 13 – Build Your AI Job Search Assistant

## Objective

Use Claude Connectors with Indeed to discover personalized job opportunities based on my profile.

## What I Did

- Created my professional profile.
- Defined my target job search criteria.
- Connected Claude with Indeed.
- Searched for matching Full Stack Developer jobs.
- Reviewed job matches and salaries.
- Analyzed required skills.
- Studied market demand.
- Identified my technical skill gaps.
- Received personalized recommendations.

- Learned how AI can automate job discovery.
- Understood the most in-demand skills for Full Stack Developers.
- Identified areas that need improvement before placements.
- Discovered companies matching my profile.
- Built a personalized AI-powered job search workflow.
