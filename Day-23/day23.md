# Day 23 – Customer & MVP Blueprint

## Objective

Used Claude AI to transform a validated startup idea into a structured Customer & MVP Blueprint by identifying customer needs, defining an MVP strategy, prioritizing features, and planning the first 30 days of execution.

---

## Startup

AI Learning Buddy for Bharat

A personalized AI-powered learning platform that delivers educational support in regional Indian languages.

---

## Report Highlights

- Executive Summary
- Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)
- Buyer Persona
- Top 10 Customer Pain Points
- Customer Journey
- Buying Triggers & Customer Objections
- MVP Recommendation
- MoSCoW Feature Prioritization
- Pricing Hypothesis
- Top 5 Risks
- 30-Day MVP Plan
- Founder Action Sheet
- Final Verdict

---

## Key Insights

- Regional-language AI tutoring addresses an underserved market.
- Customer trust and affordability are critical adoption factors.
- Building a focused MVP is more valuable than developing many features.
- Early validation with real users is essential before scaling.

---

## MVP Strategy

### Build First

- Regional language support
- Doubt-solving system
- Adaptive practice
- Parent progress dashboard

### Avoid Initially

- Gamification
- Nationwide multi-board support
- Paid acquisition
- Large feature expansion

---

## Key Learnings

- Learned how Product Managers define customer personas.
- Understood MVP planning using MoSCoW prioritization.
- Explored customer pain points and buying behavior.
- Learned how AI can assist in product strategy and startup planning.

## Conclusion

This project demonstrated how AI can assist founders in designing customer-focused MVPs, prioritizing features, reducing product risk, and creating a practical roadmap before full-scale product development.
