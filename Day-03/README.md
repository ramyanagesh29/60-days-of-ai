# 🚀 Day 3 – Role-Based Prompting

## 📖 Overview
For Day 3 of the **ABTalks 60-Day Claude Challenge**, I explored **Role-Based Prompting** and learned how assigning different personas to an AI changes the quality and perspective of its responses.

## 🎯 Objective
- Understand Role-Based Prompting
- Compare AI responses with and without assigned roles
- Analyze how different personas influence AI outputs

## 🧪 Experiment

### Question
**How should I build an AI-powered student study app?**

### Personas Used
- Without Role
- AI Educator
- Founder
- Developer

## 🔍 Key Observation

The same question produced different responses depending on the assigned role:

- **Founder Persona** focused on:
  - Problem validation
  - Target users
  - Business model
  - MVP features
  - Growth strategy

- **Developer Persona** focused on:
  - Tech stack
  - System architecture
  - AI models
  - Database
  - APIs
  - Deployment
  - Security

## 📚 Key Learning

Role-Based Prompting helps AI provide more relevant, specialized, and context-aware responses by thinking from the perspective of a specific expert.
