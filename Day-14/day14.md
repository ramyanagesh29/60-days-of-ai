# Day 14 – AI Job Red Flag Detector

## Objective

Analyze a real job description using Claude AI to identify potential risks before applying.

## What I Did

- Selected a Full Stack Developer job description.
- Collected company information.
- Used Claude AI to analyze hiring risks.
- Reviewed workplace culture indicators.
- Evaluated company reputation.
- Generated smart interview questions.

## What Claude Detected

- Overall Risk Score
- Top Red Flags
- Positive Signals
- Company Risk Analysis
- Hiring Risk Analysis
- Remote Work Validation
- Final Recommendation

## Key Learnings

- Every job should be evaluated before applying.
- AI can identify hidden warning signs in job descriptions.
- Smart interview questions help validate company culture.
- Salary transparency and clear responsibilities are important.

## Tools Used

- Claude AI
- Job Description
- GitHub
