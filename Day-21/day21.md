# Day 21 – Digital Privacy Intelligence Dashboard

## Objective

Built an AI-powered Digital Privacy Intelligence Dashboard using Claude AI to analyze a sample digital footprint and estimate privacy exposure.

---

## Features Explored

- Digital Footprint Score
- Privacy Score
- Exposure Heatmap
- Company Exposure Ranking
- Data Collection Matrix
- Risk Radar
- Digital Twin Profile
- Most Valuable Data Assets
- Privacy Improvement Plan
- Final Verdict

---

## Technologies Used

- Claude AI
- HTML
- CSS
- JavaScript
- Data Visualization

---

## Key Learnings

- Learned how AI estimates digital privacy exposure.
- Understood how online services contribute to digital footprints.
- Explored privacy risk analysis using visual dashboards.
- Learned the importance of reducing tracking and improving privacy settings.
- Understood the difference between facts and AI-generated estimates.

---

## Privacy Insights

- Multiple online services increase digital exposure.
- Parent companies can collect information across their ecosystems.
- Small privacy improvements can significantly reduce tracking risks.
